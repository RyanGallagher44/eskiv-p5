New p5.js project by Ryan Gallagher

Based on Eskiv - see reference video here

The game is simple - here are the rules:
  • You are the circle and you must collect the rectangle (use IJKL or WASD to move)

  • Each time the rectangle is collected, the following happens:

    • Your score increases by 5
    • A new rectangle is spawned
    • An enemy is introduced - touch them, the game is over

  • Each enemy has a 50/50 chance of bouncing either horizontally or vertically

  • When you reach a game over, press SPACE to play again
